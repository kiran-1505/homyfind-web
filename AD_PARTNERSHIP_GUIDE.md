### 2. Set Up a Professional Email
- Use contact@find-my-pg.com or ads@find-my-pg.com

- Looks much more professional than gmail

- Set up Google Analytics (free) BEFORE approaching anyone
- Even 500-1000 daily visitors is enough to start conversations
- Companies care about targeted audience, not just raw numbers

## Who to Approach (Priority Order)

### Tier 1: Easiest to Convert (Start Here)

#### Local Tiffin Services & Restaurants
- **Why they'll say yes**: Their customers ARE your users (PG residents)
- **How to find them**: Search "tiffin service near [PG area]" on Google Maps
- **What to offer**: City-specific ad banner for ₹1,500-2,000/month
- **Approach**: Walk in person, show your website on phone

#### Local Furniture Rental Shops
- **Why they'll say yes**: Every PG tenant needs furniture
- **How to find them**: Search on Google Maps, check JustDial
- **What to offer**: Banner ad + "Recommended" tag on listing pages
- **Approach**: Visit in person with a printout of your media kit

#### Internet/Broadband Providers (Local dealers)
- **Why they'll say yes**: PG tenants are their primary customers
- **Targets**: ACT Fibernet, Airtel, Jio local dealers
- **What to offer**: "Get WiFi" banner on PG listings without WiFi amenity
- **Approach**: Visit local dealer offices

### Tier 2: Medium Effort

#### Furniture Rental Companies (Online)
- **Targets**: Furlenco, RentMojo, CityFurnish, Pepperfry Rental
- **Contact**: Look for "advertise with us" or "partnerships" on their website
- **What to offer**: Banner ads + affiliate partnership (earn per referral)
- **Email template below**

#### Packers & Movers
- **Targets**: Porter, Dunzo, local movers
- **Why**: People relocating to PGs need moving services
- **What to offer**: Banner on search results page

#### Coaching Institutes
- **Targets**: Local coaching centers near colleges
- **Why**: Many PG tenants are students preparing for exams
- **What to offer**: City-specific banner, especially near college areas

### Tier 3: Bigger Companies (After 5000+ Daily Visitors)

#### Food Delivery
- **Targets**: Swiggy, Zomato (local marketing teams)
- **Contact**: Search "[company] advertising partnerships India"
- **What to offer**: Banner ads, sponsored recommendations

#### E-commerce
- **Targets**: Amazon, Flipkart affiliate programs
- **How**: Sign up for Amazon Associates, Flipkart Affiliate
- **What to offer**: "Essential items for your PG" section with affiliate links

#### Banks & Financial Services
- **Targets**: Paytm, PhonePe, local banks
- **Why**: Young professionals = new bank account customers
- **What to offer**: Sponsored section on homepage

## How to Approach (Step by Step)

### For Local Businesses (In Person — Most Effective)

1. **Prepare**: Print 10 copies of your media kit, have your website loaded on phone
2. **Visit during non-peak hours**: 11 AM - 1 PM or 3 PM - 5 PM
3. **Ask for the owner/manager**: "Hi, I run a website that helps PG tenants find accommodations in [city]. Many of your customers probably live in PGs nearby. Can I show you something quick?"
4. **Show your website**: Open Find-My-PG on your phone, show PG listings in their area
5. **Make the pitch**: "I can place your ad right here [point to ad spot] so that everyone searching for PGs in this area sees your business. It's only ₹1,500/month."
6. **Offer a free trial**: "I'll put your ad up for 1 week free. If you get even 1-2 customers from it, we can continue."
7. **Collect their info**: Get their business name, logo, phone number, and what they want to advertise
8. **Follow up in 1 week**: Show them analytics (clicks, impressions)

### For Online Companies (Email + LinkedIn)

1. **Find the right person**: Search on LinkedIn for "Marketing Manager at [Company]" or "Partnerships at [Company]"
2. **Connect on LinkedIn first**: Send a connection request with a short note
3. **Send email**: Use templates above
4. **Follow up**: If no response in 3-4 days, send follow-up
5. **Be persistent but respectful**: Max 3 follow-ups, then move on

## Pricing Strategy

### When You're Starting (< 1000 daily visitors)
- Offer **free trials** (1-2 weeks) to build case studies
- Charge ₹1,000-2,000/month for local businesses
- Focus on getting 5-10 advertisers, not maximizing price

### When You're Growing (1000-5000 daily visitors)
- Standard pricing: ₹3,000-5,000/month
- Offer quarterly discounts: 3 months = 10% off
- Create a self-service ad portal (future feature)

### When You're Established (5000+ daily visitors)
- Premium pricing: ₹5,000-15,000/month
- City-specific pricing (Bangalore > smaller cities)
- Annual contracts with 20% discount

## Google AdSense Setup (Passive Income)

### Requirements to Get Approved
1. Website must be live with real content
2. At least 15-20 pages of original content
3. Privacy policy page (required)
4. About us page (required)
5. Contact page (required)
6. Website must be at least 1-3 months old
7. No copyright-infringing content

### Steps
1. Go to https://adsense.google.com
2. Sign up with your Google account
3. Add your website URL
4. Place the verification code in your `<head>` tag
5. Wait for approval (usually 1-2 weeks for Indian sites)
6. Once approved, create ad units and place them on your site

### Best Ad Placements for Find-My-PG
- **Between listings**: After every 6th PG card in search results
- **Listing detail sidebar**: On desktop, right side of PG detail page
- **Mobile bottom banner**: Sticky banner at bottom on mobile
- **Search results top**: Leaderboard ad above search results

## Tracking & Reporting for Advertisers

### What to Show Advertisers Monthly
- Impressions (how many times their ad was shown)
- Clicks (how many people clicked their ad)
- Click-through rate (CTR)
- Which cities/pages got most views
- Screenshot of their ad on your site

### Free Tools to Track This
- **Google Analytics**: Page views, user demographics (free)
- **Simple click counter**: Add onClick tracking to ad links
- **Firebase Analytics**: Already integrated in your project
